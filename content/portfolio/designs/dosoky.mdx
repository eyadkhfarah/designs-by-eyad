<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>Desouky is a pioneering application that aims to connect users to a source of spiritual inspiration and tranquility through reading the Holy Quran and supplications, in addition to providing an interpretation of the Shadhiliyya Desouqiyya method. The application is designed to be a dedicated platform to come to this purpose and the definition that many Egyptians have dedicated to it, especially the generations that have surpassed the aging population.</p>
<br />
<h3>Message</h3>
<p>Providing a modern and easy digital platform that combines technology and a rich spiritual heritage, to be compatible with the connection to the Holy Quran, daily supplications, and is characterized by the Shadhiliyya Desouqiyya method, including strengthening the faith and the oasis of the soul for users.</p>
<br />
<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>For Desouky to become the primary application for fans of the Shadhiliyya Desouqiyya method and its members in companies in Egypt and abroad, and for the modern one to inspire current and future generations with the rich spiritual heritage through technology.</p>
<br />
<h3>Brand Voice</h3>
<p>Desouky's voice is characterized by calm, dignity, and simplicity, with a spiritual touch that reminds of divine greatness and the importance of tranquility. We are keen to use an Arabic language that is understandable and used with respect and spirituality, while designing the privacy of the basic points and tastes of users of the specific age group.</p>
